# Design Spec

## Colors
Almost entirely black, white, and shades of gray. The palette is:
- **Black** `#000000` — primary text, primary buttons
- **White** `#FFFFFF` — backgrounds, card fills
- **Light gray** `#F3F4F6` / `#F9FAFB` — section backgrounds, tile backgrounds
- **Border gray** `#E5E7EB` — all borders and dividers
- **Body text gray** `#374151` — paragraph text
- **Muted gray** `#6B7280` — secondary/caption text

No gradients, no brand colors, no decorative color anywhere. The only place color appears is third-party integration logos (Google, Zoom, etc.) which use their natural brand colors.

## Typography
**Inter** is the sole typeface at all weights. No serifs, no display fonts. Sans serif font

- Headings are large and bold (700 weight), dark gray/black
- Body text is regular weight (400), medium gray
- Small labels and eyebrows are medium weight (500), muted gray
- Tone is confident and direct — headings are often punchy or slightly witty

## General Style
Clean, minimal, and spacious. The design feels like a well-engineered SaaS product — professional and trustworthy without being flashy. Key traits:

- **Lots of whitespace** between every element and section
- **Cards** are white with a light gray border and a barely-there shadow — simple and flat
- **Buttons** are either solid black (primary) or outlined with a gray border (secondary). No rounded pills — just slightly rounded corners.
- **Icons** are simple stroke-style (like Lucide or Heroicons), always in gray, never filled or colorful
- Sections alternate between white and off-white backgrounds to create rhythm
- Real product UI (forms, toggles, booking cards) is embedded as visual proof points instead of abstract graphics
